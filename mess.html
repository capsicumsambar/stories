Who Created the Mess?

"Who created this mess?" Appa asked Amma.
"Not sure" said Amma.

Appa called Teju.

"Who created this huge mess - the clothes are all over the place!"
"I did not"
"Go find out if Maiu did it"

Teju goes to the backyard - "papa did you create the clothes mess"?

"Of course I did not!" said a surprised Maiu, "perhaps Kalia did?" And looked straight at Kalia who was near the backyard door

Kalia heard her name and ran to the front yard sneaking through the unlocked door

Appa decided to solve the mystery.

He first called Teju "Go and take a look at the mess and tell me what you see". Teju goes upstairs to the master bedroom. He does not see any mess. "I do not see any mess" he reports back.

Next Appa calls Maiu "Go and take a look at the mess and tell me what you see". Maiu goes upstairs to the master bedroom. She too reports back saying "I do not see any mess"

Appa calls Kalia "go check", Kalia runs up to the bedroom and checks and runs back down and sits next to Appa looking for a treat.

Appa calls Amma "can you please check the status of the mess?" Amma goes up and checks the room and the closet and says "it very messy".

The kids are surprised.

Appa says "I know who created the mess".

How did Appa know?
